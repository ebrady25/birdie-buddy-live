Good to know

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans stop at the end of the cycle; annual plans don't auto-trap you — you keep access through the term you paid for. No retention mazes.

What's the difference between Pro and All-Access?

Pro covers one sport (PGA) fully. All-Access adds NFL DFS, the lineup optimizer, the late-swap assistant, and the THEMIS simulator. Sharp adds earliest model release, raw data/model exports, and the private room.

Do you really publish your losing weeks?

Yes — the public Scorecard shows the full record, wins and misses. That's the entire point of the product.

Is my payment secure?

Billing runs through the payment processor once connected — we never store card details. Note: this preview runs in demo mode; account creation and upgrades are simulated locally until the billing backend is live.
