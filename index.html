What is BirdieBuddy?

The BirdieBuddy Index (BBI)

BirdieBuddy is a proprietary golf analytics engine that combines DataGolf's statistical projections with a custom 10-factor scoring model, course-specific adjustments, and ownership intelligence to produce superior player rankings for both season-long pools and daily fantasy sports (DFS). Unlike raw projection models, BBI layers strategic context — ownership leverage, course history multipliers, form trending, and contrarian signals — on top of pure skill metrics.

Why BBI is Different

DataGolf provides the best raw statistical projections available. BBI doesn't try to beat DG at pure skill estimation — instead, it transforms DG's output into actionable intelligence by answering the question traditional models ignore: "Given the competitive landscape, which players offer the best risk-adjusted value?" This is the gap between having data and having an edge.

Data Pipeline — 5 API Endpoints

1. Pre-Tournament Predictions

Win/Top-5/10/20/MC probabilities for full field. Source: DataGolf predictive model with 7-day recency weighting.

2. DraftKings Projections

Salary, projected fantasy points, projected ownership. Powers DFS value composite and lineup optimization.

3. Betting Market Odds

13 sportsbooks aggregated. Pinnacle as sharp book baseline. Three probability surfaces: DG Historical, DG Baseline, Market consensus.

4. Course History Rounds

7 years of round-level scores + full SG decomposition (OTT/APP/ARG/PUTT) at the specific course. Powers courseFit factor.

5. Recent Form Rounds

Last 6-9 completed PGA Tour events. Per-start SG decomposition enables form trending and category-level analysis.

DFS Value Composite

Rank Gap (40%)

Compares DataGolf skill rank vs DraftKings salary rank. Underpriced players relative to expected performance get boosted.

Formula: (salaryRank - dgRank + 30) / 60

Points per $1K (30%)

Expected fantasy output per salary dollar. Favors mid-range players with strong projected points relative to cost.

Formula: composite_score / (salary/1000)

Ownership Efficiency (30%)

Upside potential per ownership percentage point. Identifies contrarian edge — ceiling disproportionate to projected ownership.

Formula: (winPct + t5Pct × 0.5) / max(ownDfs, 0.5)
