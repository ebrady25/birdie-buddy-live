How this works. BBI is a weighted sum of ten normalized factor scores. The sliders override the weight vector, and the top-20 board is resorted on every change. The lineup distribution model assumes per-player independence — true THEMIS samples 10,000 tournaments with correlation between players in the same tee group, so this sandbox slightly under-estimates variance at the extremes.
