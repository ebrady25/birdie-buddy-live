Shadow models · HEPHAESTUS

Four challengers. Every week.

Every Monday, HEPHAESTUS runs four alternative weight configurations against the v8.5 champion. Top-20 Spearman agreement is the similarity metric. Any variant beating baseline by ≥0.03 Spearman and ≥5% Brier for 3+ consecutive weeks across 8+ events gets flagged for promotion.

Probability calibration · Brier score

How accurate the probabilities really are.

For each market, Brier score measures how close predicted probabilities land to observed outcomes (lower is better). Skill % is the improvement vs. the naive baseline of predicting the field-average rate for everyone.
